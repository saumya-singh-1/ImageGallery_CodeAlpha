How to Use the Gallery

  • Browse Images: Scroll through the gallery to see all available images
  • Filter by Category: Click category buttons (All, Nature, City, Landscape) to filter images
  • Apply Effects: Use filter buttons to change image appearance (Normal, Grayscale, Sepia, etc.)
  • View in Lightbox: Click any image to open in full-screen lightbox mode
  • Navigate: Use arrow buttons or keyboard arrows (← →) to move between images
  • Close Lightbox: Press ESC key, click X button, or click outside the image
  • Mobile Gestures: Swipe left/right on mobile devices to navigate
  • Hover Effects: Hover over images to see titles and descriptions
